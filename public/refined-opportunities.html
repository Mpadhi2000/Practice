THE CLIENT CHALLENGE

Our client was investing a lot of money into trade shows without a measurable (or guaranteed) ROI. Leads collected at the trade shows were largely unqualified because the details gathered weren’t sufficient to glean the customer’s level of purchasing intent. Leads were then passed to the sales team, who knew that the leads were unlikely to convert to sales.

THE GOALS

Refine the current lead qualification process

Introduce a lead scoring system to assign grades to each potential lead
Identify “hot” leads to pass to the sales team

Identify “warm” leads to nurture for future sales

Save the company time and money

Download Case Study

THE RESPONSEPOINT SOLUTION

Using 4,732 leads gathered at more than 17 events, ResponsePoint used the new lead qualification system and determined:

  
310 leads (7%) were given an “A” Quality Level and immediately funneled to the sales team

  
635 leads (13%) were given a “B” Quality Level and categorized in the marketing database for a nurturing campaign

  
3,787 leads (80%) were given a “C” Quality Level and immediately taken out of the lead process and were removed from all nurturing processes

   
End cost per “A” lead was $55.07, saving the client money and time, and improving the company’sROI

PAIN POINTS

  
No qualifying details

Sales team cannot determine high-priority leads.

  
Wasted time

Low-quality leads took valuable time from the sales team, costing the company ROI and opportunities to pursue better leads.

  
Poor process for lead generation

Trade show lead gen efforts weren’t producing leads that were valuable to the sales team.
